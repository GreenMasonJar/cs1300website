Building your First Gunpla

If you're a fan of lego, building things, and bad-a looking robots, you're in the right place! Gunpla is a fun, affordable, and relaxing hobby for anyone of any age.

What you need to get started:

  • Gunpla to build
  • Snipping tool
  • X-Acto knife
  • sandpaper/ sanding sticks (optional)

What Gunpla should I get?

There are many Gunpla to shoose from, and there are a multiple grades to choose from. It ranges from High Grade (HG) to Perfect Grade (PG). If you're just starting out it's recomended you start with a High Grade before moving into higher grade Gunpla.

Grade Acronym Expected Price
High Grade HG $15-$30
Real Grade RG $25-$50
Perfect Grade PG $150+
